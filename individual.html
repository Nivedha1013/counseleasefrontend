What is Individual Counseling?

Individual counseling is a personal, one-on-one therapy session designed to help you navigate emotional difficulties, mental health challenges, and life transitions. It provides a safe space to talk openly with a licensed professional.

Common Issues Addressed in Individual Counseling

Stress & Anxiety

Learn to manage daily stress, panic attacks, and overwhelming emotions.

Depression

Overcome sadness, hopelessness, and lack of motivation with expert help.

Self-Esteem Issues

Build confidence, self-worth, and positive self-image.

Trauma & PTSD

Heal from past trauma and develop coping strategies for PTSD.

Life Transitions

Navigate major life changes like job loss, relationships, or moving cities.
